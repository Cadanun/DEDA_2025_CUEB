Description:
This project analyzes global EV trends (2010-2024) using IEA data and GEO2023 reports. Visualizations show rapid EV adoption growth, with China leading sales. Clustering (K-means/hierarchical) reveals 4 market types: Leaders (China), Fast Followers (EU/US), Emerging Markets (Israel/TÃ¼rkiye), and Laggards. LDA topic modeling identifies key themes like Battery Tech and Policy Tools. Regression proves policies drive EV penetration, while battery tech boosts sales. Findings confirm infrastructure gaps in emerging markets and policy effectiveness in scaling adoption.
